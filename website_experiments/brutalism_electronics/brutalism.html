We reject the smooth surfaces of consumer electronics. Brutalism demands honesty — let the structure be the aesthetic. PCB traces are not to be hidden. Wires are not shameful. The resistor is a beautiful object.

Every component is through-hole, hand-placed, hand-soldered. We use perfboard when others use fabrication. We use wire when others use traces. We use physicality when the industry chose miniaturization.
